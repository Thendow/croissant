🥐🥐 CROISSANT 🥐🥐

A croissant is a flaky, buttery pastry known for its crescent shape and delicate layers. It is made from a laminated dough, which involves folding butter into dough multiple times to create thin, alternating layers of dough and butter. When baked, the croissant puffs up to a golden-brown color with a crisp, outer crust and a tender, airy interior. It is a classic French pastry that is often enjoyed on its own or filled with sweet or savory ingredients such as chocolate, almond paste, or ham and cheese.
